Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 4, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Focus Game ("we," "our," or "us") collects the following information:

  • Game Data: Your scores, levels achieved, and game progress (stored locally on your device)
  • Analytics Data: General usage statistics through Google Analytics
  • Advertising Data: Information used by Google AdSense to display relevant ads

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our brain training game service
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Ensure the security and proper functioning of our website

3. Data Storage

Your game progress and scores are stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to share it.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our site
  • Google AdSense to display personalized advertisements
  • Essential website functionality and user preferences

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website integrates with:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics (Privacy Policy)
  • Google AdSense: For advertising (Privacy Policy)
  • Vercel: For website hosting (Privacy Policy)

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of certain data collection
  • Data portability where applicable

9. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
